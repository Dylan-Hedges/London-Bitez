Welcome and thanks for coming to London Bitez!

We here at Bitez scour London and beyond looking for the best food around so you dont have to. Whether that be a £5 lunch or a £100 brunch, we want to have you covered.

Bitez isnt just one person, its a squad on a mission, and were slowly trying to inform London and stop the falsities we continue to read about restaurants and their food. You know what were talking about, that amazing looking restaurant that every blogger suddenly discovers and is the new best thing, yeah paid for, forget about it. We want to provide real food, real prices and real reviews.

Hopefully we can find you some London gems. Stick with us and you will see a verdict actually worth your hard earned pennies.
